In the beginning, there was nothing. Then, suddenly, out of nowhere, Bread appeared out of the void. Using its majestic powers, Bread created the
universe in which we live in. Bread created the Lords of Bread to help with the shaping of the world. Working together with their great power, Bread created
the world. While Bread created life, the Lords of Bread shaped most of the world and also created primordial beings known as the Guardians of Bread.
They then shaped the world in which we live, and Bread sprinkled life throughout the perfected world. As time passed, the mortals know Bread only as their
lord and savior. Bread is love they say, Bread is life. For we know one thing, we all live to serve Bread...
